## Draft angle

The post will unpack a short discovery call that exposed a large amount of repeated manual work. The useful lesson is not "AI can automate recruitment." It is how quickly the right workflow questions can separate busy work from judgment work.

## Intended reader

This is for service businesses that suspect their team is spending too much time on coordination, data entry, follow-up, and repeated candidate or client communication.
