Web3 Operations Agent

An AI-assisted operations system for monitoring wallets, summarising governance activity, tracking treasury changes, and producing structured research updates.

Web3 ops teams have to read everything: on-chain transfers, governance forums, multisig queues, snapshot proposals, and a dozen Discord channels. Most of it is signal-poor; the job is filtering and structuring it into something a human operator can act on in 10 minutes a day.

This is a representative concept for an AI-assisted operations agent. Specifics anonymised.

The problem

Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. The treasury lead was spending the first 90 minutes of every day reconstructing what changed overnight across 4 chains and 11 watched addresses.

Architecture

  • Indexers — chain RPC + The Graph subgraphs, normalised into a single event log.
  • Watchers — wallet, treasury, governance — each with its own deltas-only query.
  • Agent core — LLM with tool access to the event log; produces a structured daily digest.
  • Dashboard — read-only operator view; every claim links back to a tx hash or forum post.
  • Alerts — deterministic rules layer for time-critical events; the LLM never gates these.
